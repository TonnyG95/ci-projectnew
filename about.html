About Us

WHO ARE WE?

BDM Network is a new digital distributor and music publisher registred in Ireland that wants to help independent artists and labels on their music journey which can be very demanding sometimes. We think that it’s time for you to discover a new and smart way of music distributing.


Our team is full of young talented people who can and want to help you on your musical journey. We want to deliver your music to as many listeners as possible. If you are ready to raise your career to a higher level, let us know and we will be happy to help you with that. It’s time to start building your own brand with BDM Network.


BDM Network was created in 2020 with one goal: To bring a client-focused music distribution service to the table. The music industry is saturated with large distributors that place customer service on the back-burner. Here at BDM our clients are our top priority.

WHY US?

Our goal is to see your music promoted, and your earnings go as high as possible, so that we all can pay our bills.

We’re innovating a new rising model of music business. Every artist should have the same treatment. Music is all about passion, love, and creativity. We’re much more than a music distribution company, and far ahead of the old record label industry. Everything has changed. The world has changed. It’s time to think big and finally make money from your music.

PARTNERS

WHAT DO YOU GET AS ARTISTS?

Music Distribution

Distribute your music to 150+ DSPs including Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon, Beatport. Social platforms such as: Boomplay, TikTok, Triller, Tencent, NetEase...

Catalogue Management

Our proprietary technology offers an easy-to-use interface for reporting, streaming analytics, royalty payments, catalog transfer tools, split payments, user roles, and more.

Music Monetization

Increase your music revenues on YouTube, SoundCloud, Facebook/Instagram, and collect from other User Generated Content platforms.

Client Support

Our client support team answers every single email in less than one business day. It has never been easier to work with a support team that is excited to help you.

DEDICATED CLIENT SUPPORT

Your talents lie in making the music, ours lie in sharing your music with the world. We want you to have the best resources available, and use them to their fullest extent! Our support team is on hand throughout our business hours to assist you via live chat, email, social media or phone. No question is ever too big, or too small.

PERSONAL ACCOUNT MENAGER

From the start of your journey with us, we provide you with an account manager who can support you with all of your music release needs. You will have regular contact and direct access to your account manager, and they are on hand for any advice, support or assistance you might need to meet the changing demands of the music industry.

TRANSPARENT DISTRIBUTION SERVICE

When you distribute your music with BDM Network, you will know exactly what to expect from the start. You won’t ever be blindsided by unexpected music distribution fees or upload limitations. What you see if what you get! Your personal account manager will develop a release plan with you to ensure you are getting the most from our services.

COMPLATE FLEXIBELITY

You decide where you want your music to go. If you decide that you would only like to upload your music to spotify, or another individual platform, that’s not a problem! Your music is completely yours and you have the power to control exactly when and where your music releases.

DIGITAL RIGHTS MENAGMNET

We believe it is very important to protect all of your releases against copyright infringements, that is why we offer free rights management to all of music our distribution clients. This gives our artists peace of mind as their release travels the world gaining more fans and higher revenue streams.

ARTIST VERIFICATION

Making your identity clear through your social media profiles is more important than ever! Having an artist profile that is easy to recognise is vital in helping your fans find the real you. We can help our artists get their accounts verified to confirm their identity.

Verification accounts we can apply for on your behalf, free of charge:

  • YouTube Official Artist Channels (OAC)
  • Spotify
  • Soundcloud (Coming soon)
